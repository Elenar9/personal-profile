About Me

Hello my name is Danny I was born in Cali Colombia, during the time I lived in my country I studied International Economic Relations and in the year 2000 I left my country and established my residence in Madrid Spain where I worked in non-governmental organizations helping refugees to integrate, and I also did a master's degree in Diplomacy and international relations where I met wonderful colleagues and acquired knowledge that allows me to analyse the international reality today.

In 2012, I moved to London, which was a radical change in my life because for the first time I lived alone and in a multicultural and modern city, it was hard to adapt to new customs, language and change my work profile.

Today, after the pandemic, I reconsidered my professional future again because in this new world reality, a technological career gives you a wide range of possibilities and that is why I decided to study web development, which is a work challenge but also a personal one. which I hope will be successful. For this purpose, I chose the best program that is taught by CodeYourFuture(CYF), which is a non-profit organization in the United Kingdom that trains refugees and disadvantaged people to become web developers and helps them find work. in the technology industry.
